MORE ABOUT ME

  • I have been dancing for my whole life. I grew up dancing at a competitive studio training in tap, jazz, lyrical, contemporary, hip-hop, and ballet. After graduating high school, I was hired as an instructor at this same studio. Teaching dance is one of my greatest passions; it is so important and rewarding to create a safe space for kids/adolescents to connect with friends, explore their creativity, and grow in confidence!


  • I am a dancer with two pre-professional companies in the Twin Cities. I am a member of the pre-professional tap company with Kaleena Miller Dance, and I am an Apprentice with Crash Dance Productions, a concert contemporary company.

  • My family has 3 dogs who I love to play with. All 3 are rescue dogs and mixed breeds. Their names are Henry (yellow coat), Sammy (red-brown coat), and Russell (black coat). I have a cat named Peter as well!


  • I love hiking and traveling to new places. I hope to visit more National Parks and check them off my bucket list! During summer 2022 I took a road trip to Glacier National Park.
